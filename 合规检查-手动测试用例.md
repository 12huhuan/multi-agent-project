# 合规检查 — 手动测试用例

## 测试目标

验证合规审查模块的三阶段流程（政策检查 → 声明验证 → 风险报告）是否正常工作，能否正确识别 Listing 中的违规内容。

- [x] 后端已启动: http://localhost:8000
- [x] PostgreSQL / Redis / ChromaDB 运行中
- [x] LLM (DeepSeek) API Key 已配置

## 测试用例 1: 正常输入 — 合规产品（应通过）

**测试目的:** 验证一个干净的 Listing 不会被误判为违规。

### 输入数据

| 字段 | 值 |
|---|---|
| **Title** | `Premium Bluetooth Headphones Wireless` |
| **Bullet Points** | `High-quality audio with 40mm drivers`<br>`Comfortable over-ear design for all-day wear`<br>`Bluetooth 5.3 with stable connection`<br>`30 hours battery life for extended use`<br>`Built-in microphone for clear calls` |
| **Description** | `Experience immersive sound with our Premium Bluetooth Headphones. Designed for comfort and durability, these headphones deliver crisp highs and deep bass. The adjustable headband and soft ear cushions ensure a perfect fit for any head shape. Compatible with all Bluetooth-enabled devices.` |
| **Category** | `Electronics` |
| **Product Features** | `40mm drivers` |
| **Platform** | `amazon_us` |

### 操作步骤

1. 打开浏览器访问 http://localhost:5173
2. 点击左侧导航栏 **「合规审查」**
3. 在 Title 输入框中填入上述标题
4. 在 Bullet Points 输入框填入上述五点（每行一个）
5. 在 Description 输入框中填入上述描述
6. 在 Category 输入框中填入 `Electronics`
7. 在 Product Features 输入框中填入 `40mm drivers`
8. 点击 **「开始合规审查」** 按钮
9. 等待进度指示完成（约 10-30 秒）
10. 查看结果

### 预期结果

- **状态**: `awaiting_review` → `completed`
- **verdict**: `pass`
- **risk_level**: `low`
- **total_issues**: 0 或极少量（不包含 high severity）
- **critical_items**: 空数组 `[]`
- **action_items**: 空或仅含格式化建议
- **summary**: 中文摘要，内容类似"未发现严重合规问题"

## 测试用例 2: 包含违规词 — 应检测到 Policy 违规

**测试目的:** 验证政策检查 Agent 能识别禁止的声明模式。

### 输入数据

| 字段 | 值 |
|---|---|
| **Title** | `#1 Best Bluetooth Headphones - Top Rated 2026` |
| **Bullet Points** | `The best sound quality you'll ever hear`<br>`100% satisfaction guaranteed or your money back`<br>`FDA approved medical grade hearing device`<br>`Click here to buy now at the lowest price`<br>`Cure your hearing problems naturally` |
| **Description** | `Our headphones are the best on the market, guaranteed. This FDA approved device can cure tinnitus and heal your hearing. Never fail to impress with crystal clear audio. Buy now - limited time offer. We offer the cheapest price you'll find anywhere, click here to order!` |
| **Category** | `Electronics` |
| **Product Features** | `40mm drivers` |
| **Platform** | `amazon_us` |

### 操作步骤

1. 同测试用例 1 的操作步骤
2. 填入上述违规内容
3. 点击 **「开始合规审查」**
4. 等待完成
5. 查看结果

### 预期结果

- **verdict**: `violation` 或 `warning`
- **risk_level**: `high` 或 `critical`
- **total_issues**: >= 3
- **critical_items** 应包含类似以下内容：
  - `[Policy] Found prohibited term "best" in title`
  - `[Policy] Medical claim about curing/treating found`
  - `[Policy] Pricing claim "cheapest" / "lowest price" detected`
- **action_items**: 包含针对每项违规的修改建议
- **summary**: 中文总结违规情况

### API 验证（可选）

用 curl 直接调用 API 验证：

```bash
curl -X POST http://localhost:8000/api/v1/compliance/review \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "title": "#1 Best Bluetooth Headphones - Top Rated 2026",
    "bullet_points": [
      "The best sound quality you will ever hear",
      "100% satisfaction guaranteed or your money back",
      "FDA approved medical grade hearing device",
      "Click here to buy now at the lowest price",
      "Cure your hearing problems naturally"
    ],
    "description": "Our headphones are the best on the market. This FDA approved device can cure tinnitus. Buy now - limited time offer. Cheapest price anywhere!",
    "category": "Electronics",
    "product_features": ["40mm drivers"]
  }'
```

记录返回的 `task_id`，轮询状态：

```bash
# 替换 TASK_ID 为实际值
curl http://localhost:8000/api/v1/compliance/TASK_ID/status

# 当 status 为 awaiting_review 时，获取结果
curl http://localhost:8000/api/v1/compliance/TASK_ID/result
```

## 测试用例 3: 夸大声明 — 应检测到 Claim 违规

**测试目的:** 验证声明验证 Agent 能识别不实/夸大的产品声明。

### 输入数据

| 字段 | 值 |
|---|---|
| **Title** | `Wireless Earbuds with Perfect Sound Quality` |
| **Bullet Points** | `Perfect audio quality with zero distortion`<br>`50 hour battery life on a single charge`<br>`Worlds most comfortable earbuds`<br>`Better than AirPods Pro in every way`<br>`100% noise cancellation in any environment` |
| **Description** | `Experience perfect sound with zero distortion, guaranteed. Our earbuds last 50 hours and are more comfortable than any competitor. Better noise cancellation than AirPods Pro.` |
| **Category** | `Electronics` |
| **Product Features** | `13mm drivers, 6 hours battery, ANC up to 35dB` |
| **Platform** | `amazon_us` |

**关键点:** Product Features 中写明了真实的电池续航是 6 小时，而 Listing 声称 50 小时，这将触发 `exaggerated` 类型的 claim issue。

### 预期结果

- **verdict**: `warning` 或 `violation`
- **claim_issues** 应包含：
  - `unsubstantiated`: "perfect audio quality with zero distortion" 无证据
  - `exaggerated`: "50 hour battery" vs 实际 6 小时
  - `false_comparison`: "Better than AirPods Pro" 不当对比
- **critical_items** 中会包含以 `[Claim]` 为前缀的条目
